Education

Hi! My name is Cyra Moss. I am a 26 years old female developer based in Auckland, New Zealand. I am currently looking for work as a front end developer.

When I was 16 I started a computer course. I loved the idea of making my own apps. I finished level 3, 4 and 5 in business and computing courses pretty easily then I really had to think about what I wanted to do. I started searching the internet and found alot of online courses like Udemy. I started doing courses to gain knowledge for as cheap as I could as I had no money. I eventually came across SheCodes which I really wanted to do so I saved enough to get it and loved the course! SheCodes is a coding bootcamp that helped me with HTML, CSS and JavaScript along with teaching me about bootstrap, APIs, hosting, SEO, responsive and more!

I can now confidently do front end development with what I have learnt from Udemy and SheCodes. After SheCodes I was interested in full stack and wanted to learn backend too, so i started Mission Ready NZ's first Tech accelorator. Mission Ready taught me things like cloud services, AI services, Architectural patterns, Agile mindset and practices, DevOps and automated deployment, design thinking and more! Mission Ready was great and I learnt alot! But I had a break for about 6 months to look after my daughter and I am not as confident yet. I am still learning more every day.
